Title: "Echoing Dreams"

(Verse 1)
In the valleys where we wander,
Whispers beckon from afar,
With each heartbeat, we grow fonder,
Finding grace in every scar.

(Chorus)
Echoing dreams through the night,
Guiding us to what we seek,
In our journey, love will light,
Together, we are strong and meek.
